# Install Perfusion Schedule Manager on iPhone

This guide will help you install the Perfusion Schedule Manager as an app on your iPhone.

## Prerequisites

- iPhone with iOS 11.3 or later
- Safari browser (required for installation)
- Access to the hosted website URL

## Installation Steps

### Step 1: Open in Safari
1. Open **Safari** on your iPhone (this must be done in Safari, not Chrome or other browsers)
2. Navigate to your Perfusion Schedule Manager website URL
3. Log in to verify the app is working correctly

### Step 2: Add to Home Screen
1. Tap the **Share** button at the bottom of Safari (box with arrow pointing up)
2. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
3. You'll see a preview with the app name "Perfusion Schedule"
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner

### Step 3: Launch the App
1. Return to your iPhone home screen
2. You'll see the Perfusion Schedule app icon
3. Tap the icon to launch the app
4. The app will open in full-screen mode, just like a native app!

## Features When Installed

- **Full-screen experience** - No Safari browser bars
- **Home screen icon** - Quick access like any other app
- **Offline access** - View cached data even without internet
- **Fast loading** - Improved performance with local caching

## Updating the App

The app will automatically update when you open it and new changes are available. No need to reinstall!

## Troubleshooting

### "Add to Home Screen" option not showing
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Make sure you're on iOS 11.3 or later

### App shows old data after update
1. Open the app
2. Pull down to refresh
3. If issues persist, delete the app and reinstall

### Need to uninstall?
1. Long-press the app icon on your home screen
2. Tap "Remove App"
3. Confirm deletion
